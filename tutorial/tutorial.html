Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour (unsifted)
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup butter at room temperature
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar
  • 3 mashed ripe bananas
  • 1 teaspoon milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
    •

    Instructions

    1. 1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees. Spray a loaf pan with non-stick cooking spray or grease with butter and set aside.
    2. 2. Add the bananas to a bowl and mash with a fork.
    3. 3. Add butter to same bowl and mix together.
    4. 4. Add the vanilla extract, egg, and milk to the bowl and use the same fork to mash and stir until no yellow streaks of egg remain.
    5. 5. In a second large bowl, whisk together the flour, sugar, baking soda, and salt.
    6. 6. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix together with a spatula just until combined.
    7. 7. Pour the batter into prepared loaf pan and bake for 45-55 minutes until a toothpick inserted in the center of the bread comes out clean.
