What Can Be Added to a Banana Bread?

Perhaps you'd like to create a twist on such a simple recipe. Here's what you can add to a banana bread to give it some extra flavor!

  • Nuts! Pecans can be added for a bit of sweetness. Add almonds for that savory taste. And for a classic combination: try adding walnuts.
  • Sour Cream! Sour cream or Greek yogurt add a bit of a tart taste.
  • Dried fruits! Feel free to add raisins, dried cranberries, apricots, or dates for a fun twist.
  • Chocolate chips! Whenever I make banana bread, I always add chocolate chips. It's the best way to have it!
  • Trail Mix! Can't make up your mind on what you want to add in? Just throw everything in!
  • Maple Syrup! Adding maple syrup really helps banana bread feel like the perfect breakfast snack.
  • Nutella! Try swirling nutella into the mix from the start.
  • Caramel! Want your banana bread to feel more like a dessert? Try infusing it with caramel and adding a brown sugar glaze on top.

    Ingredients!

  • 4 ripe bananas, mashed
  • Splash of vanilla
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil, melted
  • 1 3/4 flour
  • 1/3 cup brown sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 chocolate chips (vegan)
    •

    Instructions!

      1. Mix mashed bananas, vanilla, and melted coconut oil together in a bowl. Stir well.
      2. In a separate bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and baking soda. Stir until well combined.
      3. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix to combine.
      4. Pour into a parchment paper-lined loaf pan and bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 1 hour or until browned and cracked on top.
